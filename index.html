About Beach Volleyball

Slammers Beach Volleyball offers professional volleyball competitions, a licenced bar with a happy hour from 6 to 7p.m , and a fitness for youths(15-30 years old). To be able to join,register your team below

Benefits of registering a team:

  1. New teams get a 20% discount on registration for the next season.
  2. During August only, you can purchase 4 drinks for the price of 3 at the bar.
  3. The nw season starts August 21st and you can get your nominations in for the next 4-a-side weekend competition.
